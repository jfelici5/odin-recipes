Curry in a Klay Pot

Description

This is a great dish to share among friends! The combination of Curry in a Klay pot is phenomenal and creates a creamy, smooth taste that is excellent to the tongue. What a treat!!!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb of chicken
  • 2 tbsp of Indian Curry powder
  • 8 onion rings
  • 2 3-inch "trophies"
  • Infinite splashes of "Brother" sauce
  • 1 Klay Pot

Steps

  1. Take your chicken and bake it in a Davidson pan
  2. Add the indian Curry powder to the chicken
  3. Toss in the 8 onion rings and mix until crispy
  4. Add the trophies for decoration
  5. Add a splash of "Brother" sauce and serve in a Klay pot
