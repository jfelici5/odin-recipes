Fried Durantula

Description

What a versatile dish this one is!!! Fried Durantula is simply a the most unique dish one could find. You can serve it with anything, and it will always taste great!!! It pairs extremely well with snake, if you have it

Ingredients

  • 1 head of Oklahoma snake
  • 4 cupcakes
  • 2 tbsp "female dog" sauce
  • 1 live tarantula
  • 2 onion rings

Steps

  1. Take your oklahoma snake and marinade it with love for 10 years
  2. Cut the head off of the snake, and rub the 4 cupcakes on it
  3. Plate the snake with 2 onion rings
  4. Deep fry a large tarantula and serve with snake
  5. Enjoy!! (Pairs very well with Curry in a Klay Pot)
