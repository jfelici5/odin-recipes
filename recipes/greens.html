Draymond Greens

Description

This dish is a super healthy snack that will add value to any dish. Although it is sometimes too gritty in texture and might have a certain weird taste, it has health effects that reach far beyond what you would expect, and it has a certain knack for raising the level of the ingredients around it

Ingredients

  • 1 defensive greens
  • 4 onion rings
  • 5 tbsp of "AND ONEEE" sauce
  • 3 chicken leg (kicks)

Steps

  1. Wash the defensive greens, scrubbing out as much grit as possible
  2. Add the greens to a pan, cook on high heat
  3. When the greens have reduced, add the 3 chicken legs in a flailing motion
  4. Add the onion rings upon completion of the legs, stir until crispy
  5. Serve with AND ONEEE sauce and enjoy!
